# Escalation & SLA Policy

Tickets flagged as high severity or SLA-risk must never be auto-responded to.
The agent's role for these is to draft a response for human review and flag the
ticket for escalation — never to send anything directly.

A ticket is SLA-risk if it has been open more than 12 hours without a first
response, or more than 48 hours without resolution.

Angry or legally-sensitive language (e.g. mentions of lawsuits, chargebacks,
regulatory complaints) always triggers high-severity classification and human
escalation, regardless of the underlying issue's apparent size.
