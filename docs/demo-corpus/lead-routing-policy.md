# Lead Routing Policy

New leads are scored on company size, source, and stated budget. Leads from paid
channels (ads, sponsored content) are prioritized over organic/referral leads for
initial response time, but all leads must receive a first-touch email within one
business day.

Enterprise leads (self-reported company size 500+) should always get a read step
to confirm existing HubSpot contact/deal history before drafting any outreach —
never draft a "welcome" email to an existing customer.

Round-robin assignment is deterministic and handled outside the agent — the agent
should never attempt to pick a specific sales rep by name.
