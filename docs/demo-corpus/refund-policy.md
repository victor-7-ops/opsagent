# Refund & Cancellation Policy

Customers may request a full refund within 14 days of purchase, no questions asked.
After 14 days, refunds are issued only for verified service outages exceeding 24
continuous hours, prorated to the affected period.

Refund requests must never be auto-approved by an agent. All refund actions require
human approval regardless of amount — this is a high-risk category by default.

Cancellations take effect at the end of the current billing period. No partial-month
refunds are issued for cancellations.
